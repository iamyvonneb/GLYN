About Us

Glyn Boutique is a Women's clothing store based in Cabuyao, Laguna. It is owned by three (3) ladies who are still in college. They establieshed this clothing business to support their financial needs. Glyn Boutique also offers different trendy tops at affordable price as well as tote bags with their own design.
